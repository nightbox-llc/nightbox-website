# Nightbox LLC commits to the EFF Do Not Track (DNT) Policy v1.0 for
# https://nightboxllc.com.
#
# Summary:
#   - We honor the DNT request header from any visitor.
#   - We do not log or retain identifiable information about visitors who
#     send DNT, beyond minimal operational telemetry (HTTP status counts).
#   - We do not share, sell, or rent visitor data with third parties.
#   - We do not embed third-party trackers, advertising pixels, or fingerprinting
#     scripts.
#   - We use first-party static-site infrastructure (Vercel Edge) and a single
#     first-party agent endpoint (api/chat.js) which does not log identifiable
#     content from DNT visitors.
#
# Discrepancies between this policy and operational reality should be reported
# to security@nightboxllc.com or via the coordinated-disclosure pipeline at
# https://nightboxllc.com/.well-known/security.txt.
#
# This policy adheres to the format defined at:
