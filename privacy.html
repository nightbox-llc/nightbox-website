Privacy Policy

Nightbox LLC ("Nightbox", "we", "us") respects the privacy of visitors to nightboxllc.com and parties that contact us through any channel. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

Information we collect

  • Direct contact information: name, email, organization, message content when you email us, fill a form, or use the chat.
  • Technical information: IP address, user agent, referrer (standard server logs).
  • Chat agent transcripts: retained for service improvement; not used for training models.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry or correspondence.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve nightboxllc.com and the chat agent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your contact information with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not use chat transcripts to train external AI models.

Third-party processors

  • Google Workspace — email infrastructure
  • Vercel — site hosting and Edge runtime
  • Anthropic / Vercel AI Gateway — chat agent inference

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing legal@nightboxllc.com. Residents of California (CCPA), the European Union (GDPR), and other jurisdictions with comparable rights are entitled to those rights in full.

Data retention

Direct contact emails are retained indefinitely as part of our business correspondence record. Chat transcripts are retained for 90 days unless required for an active engagement. Server logs are retained for 30 days.

Security

We operate behind enterprise-grade email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC strict, TLS-RPT, MTA-STS) and serve all content over HTTPS with HSTS preload-eligible policy. Coordinated security disclosure: see /.well-known/security.txt.

Changes

We will post changes to this policy at nightboxllc.com/privacy with a revised effective date.
